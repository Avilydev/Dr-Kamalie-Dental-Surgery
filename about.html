About Dr Kamalie

Dr Emile Ambrose Kamalie is a qualified dentist in Muizenberg and Strandfontein who obtained his Bachelor of Dental Surgery from the University of the Western Cape. He works with patients of all ages and offers sedation for children to make dental visits more comfortable.

Dr Kamalie opened his practice in Strandfontein at the age of 27 and has recently expanded his services to Muizenberg, allowing him to care for more patients across the community.

Outside of the practice, Dr Kamalie has a few interests that patients often enjoy hearing about. He is a dedicated Arsenal supporter, takes pride in his sneaker collection, and keeps up with the latest anime and manga. He also enjoys spending time around the braai with family and friends.

Above all, Dr Kamalie is family-oriented. When he is not working, he is a husband and a father to three girls, including one very special pitbull.
