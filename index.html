Community-Focused Dental Care For The Whole Family

Dr Emile Kamalie, a dentist in Muizenberg and Strandfontein, provides comprehensive dental care for patients of all ages. From routine check-ups and restorative dentistry to orthodontic and aesthetic treatments, we provide various dental treatments. Our goal is simple: we aim to help you maintain healthy teeth and feel confident about your smile.

Children, teens, and adults are all welcome. Sedation options are also available for patients who feel anxious about dental visits or for younger patients who may need extra comfort during treatment.

Meet Dr Emile Kamalie

Dr Emile Ambrose Kamalie is a qualified general dentist in Muizenberg and Strandfontein who also provides orthodontics and cosmetic dentistry. He works with patients of all ages and aims to provide comfortable dental care for anxious patients or children through sedation options.

Outside of dentistry, Dr Kamalie is very much a family man. When he is not in his dental practice, he is a husband and father to three girls, including one very special pitbull.
