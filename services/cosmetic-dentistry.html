Cosmetic Dentistry in Muizenberg & Strandfontein

Your smile is often one of the first things people notice about you. Small details, such as a chipped edge, uneven spacing, or worn enamel, can slowly affect the way you feel about your teeth. Cosmetic dentistry focuses on improving the appearance of your smile while still respecting the natural structure of your teeth.

Dr Kamalie offers cosmetic dental treatments at his practices as a dentist in Muizenberg and Strandfontein, helping patients make subtle changes that can refresh the look of their smiles.

What is cosmetic dentistry?

Cosmetic dentistry focuses on improving the appearance of teeth while keeping your smile natural and balanced. Treatment plans often start with a conversation about what you notice when you look in the mirror.

You might want to address:

  • Small chips or rough edges
  • Gaps between certain teeth
  • Teeth that appear uneven in shape
  • Discolouration that does not respond to routine cleaning

Before cosmetic work begins, Dr Kamalie may first check the health of your teeth during a routine examination or cleaning appointment. Maintaining healthy gums and teeth creates a stable foundation for aesthetic treatments.

How does composite bonding improve my smile?

Composite bonding is often used to reshape or rebuild small areas of a tooth. A tooth-coloured material is carefully placed and shaped to blend with the surrounding enamel.

Blending may help to address:

  • Minor chips
  • Small gaps
  • Uneven edges
  • Areas where enamel has worn down

Because the material is shaped directly on the tooth, adjustments can often be made in a single visit. Patients sometimes combine bonding with other treatments later on, especially if they are considering clear aligners to improve the position of their teeth.

What are composite veneers?

Composite veneers cover the front surface of a tooth to change its appearance. They can improve the shape, size, or visible colour of your teeth that stand out when you smile.

This option may be considered if you would like to address:

  • Uneven tooth proportions
  • Worn enamel
  • Teeth that appear slightly misaligned

During your consultation, Dr Kamalie will look closely at your bite and surrounding teeth. In some situations, restorative work, such as fillings or crowns, may first be needed to support the health of the tooth.

What is composite injection moulding?

Composite injection moulding is a technique used to shape composite material across several teeth with the help of a custom mould. This approach can help create more uniform shapes and smoother contours.

Patients sometimes explore this option when they want to refine the overall appearance of their smile. It can be especially helpful when small adjustments are needed across multiple teeth.

Maintaining your results still relies on good oral care. Regular check-ups and professional scale and polish appointments help keep your teeth clean and support the longevity of cosmetic treatments.

Is cosmetic dentistry right for me?

Cosmetic dentistry often begins with a simple conversation. You may want to refine the shape of a tooth, close a gap, or smooth edges that have been worn over time.
